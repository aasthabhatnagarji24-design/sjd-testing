How We Purify Your Water

Our plant is built to give you safe, clear, and good-tasting water every single day.

2000 LPH Plant Capacity

Our plant can treat up to 2000 litres of water per hour. This high capacity helps us serve many homes and societies across Sanchi and Salamatpur without any drop in water quality.

RO + UV + TDS + pH Checks

Water passes through RO membranes, UV treatment, and other modern filters. We keep a close eye on TDS (dissolved solids) and pH levels so the water is completely safe and pleasant to drink.

Food-Grade Jars & Campers

We use premium food-grade plastic jars and campers that are thoroughly washed and sanitized inside automated cleaning lines before refilling. Each jar is checked for micro-cracks and leaks.

Regular Plant Maintenance

Our team follows a strict daily cleaning and maintenance schedule for the plant membranes. This keeps our purification systems running healthily and prevents supply breakdowns.

About Shri Jal Dhara

Pure water, clear records, honest service

Shri Jal Dhara is a bottled water supplier based in Sanchi, Madhya Pradesh. We run a 2000 LPH RO + UV plant and deliver 20L jars and campers to homes, housing societies, offices, and events. Our goal is simple: give you safe drinking water on time, with clear jar tracking and friendly support.

How We Started

Shri Jal Dhara started as a local water jar supplier. We saw daily issues with unclear quality and irregular delivery across our neighborhood.

Established on March 16, 2021, Shri Jal Dhara has become synonymous with purity. For the past 5 years, we have been continuously supplying high-quality drinking water to the region through our 2000 LPH plant.

Today, we serve homes, offices, and small businesses with a simple, transparent system that puts purity first and keeps water delivery reliable.

🌟 Special Service: Dedication in the Corona Era Since our journey began on March 16, 2021, we have not given up even in the face of the daunting challenges of the Corona pandemic. Despite the lockdown and restrictions, we ensured the timely availability of pure drinking water in the area while adhering to strict safety standards. Serving society during this time of crisis is our greatest achievement and a matter of pride.
